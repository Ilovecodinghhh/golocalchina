## 5. How the Service Works

5.1 **Discovery.** Tourists browse Guide Service Listings. Each listing is authored by, and represents the views and proposals of, the individual Guide.

5.2 **Connection Request.** A Tourist submits a Connection Request specifying date, party size, language, and any requested adjustments to the Guide's proposed plan.

5.3 **Acceptance.** Guides may accept or decline freely. Upon acceptance, a **Guiding Service Contract** is formed directly between Tourist and Guide. The Platform is not a party.

5.4 **Payment.**
   (a) Tourist pays the full quoted amount (Guide price + 12% Information Service Fee) to the PSP at the time of acceptance.
   (b) Funds are held by the **PSP**, not by the Platform.
   (c) Following service completion (or per Guide-set milestone schedule), the PSP disburses the Guide price (in CNY) to the Guide, and the Information Service Fee to the Platform.
   (d) Tourists transact in their card currency; FX is handled by the PSP at the rate disclosed at checkout.

5.5 **Performance.** The Guide performs the service directly. The Platform does not supervise, dispatch, schedule, or otherwise control the Guide's work.

5.6 **Completion and review.** After the service date, both parties may submit reviews.
